职位描述：

RESPONSIBILITIES:

• Be able to analyze popular B2B or B2C e-commerce sites user experience based on internet trends, conceptualize, create and execute interactive design concepts and produce elegant and simple user friendly system interface in line with user requirements.
• Work closely with product manager to understand requirements and produce interactive UI design, wireframes, mock-ups and cutting HTML templates. A fair amount of information architecting is required
• Responsible for the front end user design of the company international B2B or B2C business e-commerce platform.
• Responsible for the visual effect of interface design, layout of model functions
• Responsible for the web page’s or mobile application UI/UX design and UI/UX design proposal.
• Responsible for continually improving the usability, usefulness, and desirability of business-oriented e-commerce portal.
• Be a self-starter with a can-do attitude that enables you to work independently.
• Accomplish other tasks assigned by the management.

QUALIFICATIONS AND / OR EXPERIENCE REQUIRED:

• Minimum a Professional Certificate, Diploma, Advanced/Higher/Graduate Diploma, Bachelor's Degree, Post Graduate Diploma or Professional Degree in either Computer Science, Art & Design, Advertising/Media, Human Factors Engineering or equivalent.
• Extensive experience in using UX design best practices to design solutions, and a deep understanding of mobile-first and responsive design.
• Ability to iterate your designs and solutions efficiently and intelligently.
• Excellent UI design skills and has a demonstrable track record of designing successful interfaces for websites
• A clear understanding of the importance of user-centered design.
• Expertise in UI/UX software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Axure and etc.
• Sensitive to typography, color, and UI design.
• Experience in HTML5 & CSS3 is a plus.
• E-commerce web page design experience, experience in foreign trading and international trading will be advantage.
• Good aesthetic and creative ability, diligence, strong learning ability, strong sense of responsibility, positive, proactive, good team spirit and professional ethic.

光汇石油集团简介
    光汇石油成立于1992年，是国内知名的民营石油企业集团，被誉为仅次于“三桶油”的国内第4大石油集团。2008年在香港联交所主板上市（证券代码HK0933），2017年营业收入超500亿港元。集团主要从事上游油气田开发开采和生产、远洋油轮运输、石油仓储与码头以及石油国际贸易与海上供油、成品油批发零售等业务。大陆、香港、新加坡三地拥有员工超过2000人。2017年，在全球石油天然气企业中排名第58位、成为中国大陆入榜民营油企排名最高的公司。在2017《财富》中国500强排名中，光汇石油排名第139名，位居民营石油企业首位，排名仅次于“三桶油”。
      光汇石油拥有迪那1 气田、吐孜气田以及渤海湾曹妃甸油田区块三个油气田项目；拥有四艘Aframax远洋油轮、五艘超大型油轮（VLCC），总容量超过200 万载重吨。集团正在建设位于舟山外钓岛的316 万立方米库容的石油仓储设施。该码头将配备13个从千吨级到30 万吨级大小不等的泊位。同时，光汇还在兴建位于大连长兴岛上总库容达719 万立方米的石油仓储设施，该码头将配备13个从千吨至30 万吨级大小不等的泊位。光汇石油集团也是中国最大的海上供油服务供货商之一，服务已扩展全球港口，贸易产品种类包括燃料油、原油、汽油及石油化工和相关石油制品等。集团将继续大力开拓发展上游业务，从事油气田开发开采和生产，锐意成为知名的国际能源资源型企业。
      依托庞大的实体经济体，2016年1月，光汇石油创新性地推出了“石油产业链+互联网”的综合电子商务平台——光汇云油，通过提供大众储油、云储油、储油通、海上供油在线平台等系列产品和服务，进一步整合石油采购、供应、储存、物流服务、金融服务等，打造全产业链的石油能源电商生态圈，引领石油能源行业创新发展。
