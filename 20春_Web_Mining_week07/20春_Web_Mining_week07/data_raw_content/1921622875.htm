职位描述：

Job description: 
1. In charge of the SW project portfolio and help to improve the user experience and WoM for MIUI SW relevant experience
2. Manage SW integration implementation and work with Mi phone Software team and all the relevant internet services teams to best deliver qualified software for Xiaomi Android devices
3. Cross-functional communication with business development teams and technical account teams to finalize the software project spec.
4. Work with global software product managers to manage software product development schedule, optimize internal processes to achieve the business goal
5. Work with Global business development team and follow up the cooperation porjects with KAs
 
Job requirements:
1. Fluent English, bachelor degree or above
2. Working in smartphone/IOT industry for operators or retailors is preferred, more than 2 years of working experience in the same position is preferred
3. Having successful software project management experience and focusing on product details and user experience
4. Extensive connections in software implementation on consumer electronics and smartphone industry
5. Have strong comprehension on market trend, business model and good partner and team communication skills
6. Passionate, strong sense of teamwork, good at cooperating with people
