职位描述：

工作职责:
1、负责国际化产品用户体验分析和专题研究，含：不同市场用户需求和特性分析、竞品分析、产品全流程体验等。
2、负责海外平台全流程用户体验项目推动。确定项目方向，引领项目进程，确保项目沟通、控制和验收；
3、相关流程、产品的设计，与产品、技术及其他部门沟通协调；
4、负责相关服务直接提升。
Key role and Responsibilities
1. Conduct in-depth research on user experience about internationalized products from various angles, including but not limited to different user demands in different markets, competitor analysis, full process experience, etc.
2. Drive overseas user experience projects to completion. Determine the direction of the project, lead the whole process, including communication, control and acceptance of the process
3. Communicate and coordinate with other departments
4. Be responsible for the direct promotion of related services


任职资格:
1、有海外工作/学习经验，了解当地用户需求和特性，语言流利；（日语韩语及英语方向）
2、3年及以上国际化互联网公司产品经验，PO/前端/用户增长PM优先；
3、有海外OTA行业工作经验优先
4、以结果为导向，抗压能力强，善于沟通，推动目标达成。
5、能深入分析问题，逻辑思维/数据分析能力优异，用户和产品Sense好。
6、热爱产品工作，持续学习、了解市场/用户/行业，自驱动成长。
Key Qualifications and Experience
1. Full professional proficiency in English. Native speakers preferred.
/ Native or proficient Japanese speaker with a strong grasp of Chinese or English. / Native or proficient Korean speaker with a strong grasp of Chinese or English.
2. 3+ years’ online and mobile product management or product design experience
3. Overseas OTA product management experience is a big plus
4. Strong initiative and result orientation. Ability to work independently under limited resources and to initiate clear courses of actions. Ability to deliver quality results under time pressure.
5. Deep understanding of product, good analytical skills and logical thinking.
6.Be passionate about work; continue to learn and understand the market/users; have self-motivation
