职位描述：

We're looking for talented content developers for China's leading language learning app. As a content developer, you will write and workshop content, help direct graphics and animations, and study data to iterate our course.

Our content developers are passionate about language. They are avid readers and storytellers with flare for dialogue. And they have a great sense of humor, too.

At the same time, they're obsessed with details, so grammar nerds are welcome! Our team loves to write, but we're also careful editors, and know that the best work comes through revision.

If you can balance creativity with attention to detail, please send us your resume!

Note: This position is for both Chinese and native English speakers.

Responsibilities

Design interactive content in English and Chinese for beginner, intermediate, and advanced level users
Regularly test and iterate content based on data and user feedback
Review and edit content according to product and style guidelines
Deploy content on our content management system
Help conduct user surveys
Collaborate with product managers to develop and refine product features
Help manage audio-visual content (i.e., work alongside our graphic design team and voice actors)
Qualifications

Excellent English language skills
A passion for culture and language
A love of good stories
Obsessed with details
Proactive, responsible, and organized
Open-minded and collaborative
Language assessment experience preferred
Education experience preferred (familiarity with CEFR highly preferred)
Writing experience highly preferred
Understanding of syllabus design a plus
Editing experience a plus

企业介绍：

流利说®（NYSE：LAIX）是领先的人工智能驱动的教育科技公司，由王翌博士和胡哲人、林晖博士于2012年9月共同创立。作为智能教育的倡行者，流利说®拥有一支业内领先的人工智能团队，其自主研发的人工智能英语老师，基于深度学习技术，能够为每一位用户提供个性化、自适应的学习课程。

经过多年积累，流利说®已拥有巨型的“中国人英语语音数据库”。在此基础上，公司自主研发了领先的英语口语评测、写作打分引擎和深度自适应学习系统，致力于为用户提供一整套系统性的英语学习解决方案，从听、说、读、写多个维度提升用户的英语水平。

2018年9月，由于品牌升级，公司正式启用“LAIX”作为集团化运营的新名称。LAIX 前三个字母意为：Learning + AI，以人工智能驱动教育，引导新的学习方式；而“X”则代表着未知，寓意解锁人生的无限可能。

流利说®旗下目前拥有包括“流利说®英语”、“流利说®雅思”在内的英语教育类 APP，以及“流利说®懂你英语”、“流利说®阅读”、“地道发音课”等英语学习产品。截止2019年3月31日，旗舰App“流利说®英语”的注册用户数已超过 1.2 亿人，付费学员超过 250 万人，覆盖全球 175 个国家，全国 384 个城市，亦拥有国内领先的英语学习 App 和活跃的语言学习社区，曾被苹果公司评为“App Store精华”和“2013年度精选 App”。

2018年9月27日，流利说®以股票交易代码“LAIX”正式挂牌纽交所，以其开发的教育 3.0 模式，被誉为“AI+教育”第一股。上市前，流利说®曾获得IDG、挚信资本、GGV、华人文化产业投资基金 (CMC)、心元资本、双湖资本、赫斯特资本等知名VC的多轮投资。
