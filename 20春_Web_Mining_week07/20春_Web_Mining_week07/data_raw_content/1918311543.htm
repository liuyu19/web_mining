职位描述：

工作职责
1、负责产品的界面设计，为新功能新产品提供创意方案。
2、参与需求前期讨论，与产品经理、交互设计师、开发工程师沟通协作，从用户体验的角度提出建议及解决方案。
3、配合开发工程师进行用户界面的实现，跟踪产品效果，保证设计质量。
4、懂数据，能通过用户需求的挖掘与数据的分析，给业务带来体验端的价值拓宽。
5、负责研究设计趋势，推动创新方案，实现产品界面呈现在用户体验上的突破，总结设计方法论。

任职要求
1、本科及以上学历，有2年以上工作经验，热爱设计，英语沟通流利有大型互联网公司经验者优先。
2、性格开朗，有较强的责任心，对设计充满热情和兴趣。
3、有良好的团队协作沟通能力，敢于接受挑战和承担工作压力。


Job Responsibilities
1、Take charge of the UI design of the product and provide creative proposals for new products and new functions when needed.
2、Engage in preliminary discussions for different requirements, collaborating with product managers, other designers and developers, offering advice and potential solutions from the user experience perspective.
3、Work with developers to achieve the optimal UI design, track key metric after products go online, and maintain the design quality if any changes are needed.
4、Have an understanding of data analysis and the ability to improve the product by digging deeply into users' requirements and analyzing relevant data.
5、Conduct research about design trends and help promote creative proposals, improve UI design from a user experience perspective, and analyze different design methods.

Job Requirements
1、Bachelor degree or higher with 2+ years of relevant work experience. Priority will be given to those fluent in English with experience working in a large internet company
2、Have an out-going, open-minded personality, strong sense of responsibility, and must always be passionate about design.
3、Have good communication and teamwork skills, and must always be ready to take on challenges at work
