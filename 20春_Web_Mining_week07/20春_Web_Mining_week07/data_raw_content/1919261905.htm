职位描述：

Xiaomi Global Operator Management team in Beijing is looking for a talented professional who can lead operator requirement management efforts globally. In this role, you will directly interface to global customer with technical requirement collection, and you will lead an internal cross-functional team to drive the requirement analysis, feature development plan, and to offer applicable product integration plan, help to improve the user experience on our smartphone portfolio.
To ensure an applicable product integration plan, you will be familiar with the global operator requirement trend and technical roadmap in both short term and long term, you will be experienced on negotiation the requirement scope and plan with customers.

Description
• In charge of the global operator feature planning and help to improve the customer satisfaction and user experience for smartphone which to delivered in the global operator marketing.
• Work with Sales, BD and account team to communicate with customers on both operator requirement & technical roadmap and our smartphone solutions, and offer applicable product integration plan.
• To be the operator feature owner, work with internal development functions to make feature development plan, manage SW integration implementation and make sure the product delivery on product in short term or long term according to the plan.

Key Qualifications
• Fluent English, bachelor degree or above
• Working in smartphone/IOT industry for operators or retailors is preferred, more than three years of working experience in the same position is preferred
• Having operator requirement management experience and focusing on requirement management details and feature planning
• Extensive connections in software implementation on consumer electronics and smartphone industry
• Have strong comprehension on market trend, good partner and team communication skills
• Passionate, strong sense of teamwork, good at cooperating with people
