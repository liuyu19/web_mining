职位描述：

职责描述：
- Analyze customer functional safety requirement
- Propose technical safety concept on system level together with system manager
- Create HSI (Hardware Software Interface) together with Hardware architects
- Make SW safety strategies and participate in SW safety architect design
- Implement some of functional safety components from SW side for in vehicle controllers or graphic controllers
- Collaborate with global team (USA, Germany, India, …) to fulfill common safety goals
- Participate in functional safety activities and help safety manager to document SW safety tasks
- Participate in Agile/SAFe development activities
- Make safety training to other software developers

任职要求：
- Bachelor/Master in computer science, EE engineering, automation and similar area
- At least 5 years SW development experience in embedded products or SW tools
- Good C/C++ programming skills
- Experience with ISO26262/functional safety in existing projects for safety related products (EPS, steering, Instrument Cluster…)
- Experienced in SW development under different OS, for example embedded Linux, RTOS(QNX), Android
- Familiar with some HMI/UI tools (e.g. QT, Kanzi) is preferred
- Knowledge/experience in AUTOSAR
- Experience with delivering/merging/baselining source code under SVN, Git, RTC, etc.
- Familiar with script languages (e.g. shell, python…) is preferred
- Be able to analyze/optimize system performance in function safety domain
- Understanding on graphic rendering and knowledge in OpenGL is a plus
- Knowledge/experience in system virtualization/hypervisor is a plus
- English communication capability is a must, and fluent written/oral communication is preferred
- Independent development capability and strong sense of responsibility
