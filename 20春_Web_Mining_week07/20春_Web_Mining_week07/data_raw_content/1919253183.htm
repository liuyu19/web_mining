职位描述：

Job description:
1. In charge of the SW product portfolio for global market and help to improve the user experience for smartphone and MIOT SW relevant experience.
2. Effectively carry out competitor analysis, market research, demand collection, industry development foresight, and propose pragmatic, comprehensive product plan.
3. Work closely with other product team, project team to improve product experience and manage SW implementation and make sure the project delivery.

Job requirements:
1. Working in smartphone industry is preferred, more than two years of working experience in the same position is preferred
2. Passionate, strong sense of smartphone and consumer electronics product, having successful product management experience and focusing on product details and user experience
3. Extensive connections in software implementation on smartphone and consumer electronics industry.
4. Have strong comprehension on market trend, good partner and team communication skills
5. Fluent English, bachelor degree or above
