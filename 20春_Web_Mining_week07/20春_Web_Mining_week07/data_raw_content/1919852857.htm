职位描述：

As a BD manager, the ultimate mission is to grow Xiaomi global internet service business and to enrich the user experience of our MIUI products. On a day to day basis, the person needs to drive our product teams to propose product ideas and thus to identify and develop the potential collaboration opportunities with strategic partners.

Responsibilities
Ø Manage corporate level relationship with strategic partners like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Yandex, Facebook… etc
Ø Identify collaboration opportunities between companies and partners in various internet services solutions
Ø Develop win-win strategies with partners by exploring new business models that generate substantial revenues and/or gain new users
Ø Collaborate with internal teams and to achieve the overall business goals among smartphone and mobile internet services business
 
Qualifications
Ø Minimum 4 years experiences in software business development or software account management
Ø Experiences in hi-tech companies or internet services companies, having strong knowledge in global (or a specific region) software/mobile internet service industries
Ø Excellent presentation skills and inter-personal skills are required
Ø Bilingual in Mandarin Chinese and English is must
Ø Software development experienced is preferred
